About the Game

Origin Story

NFT Battle Arena was born from the desire to create something more than just another game. We observed how popular mini-apps like Hamster Kombat gathered millions of users, but quickly disappeared due to lack of real value and gameplay depth.

Our team decided to take a different path: combining strategic gameplay with Web3 technologies, where every player has real ownership of their NFT characters and can earn real TON tokens.

Why we won't repeat their fate?

We focus on long-term value: unique battle mechanics, NFT ownership, social features, and real rewards.

For Players

How to Start Playing

1. Launch the bot: Go to @NFT_Battle_Arena_bot in Telegram
2. Get free NFT: Choose one of two starter characters
3. Learn mechanics: Play with bot to understand battle system
4. Earn BP: Complete tasks and battle for Battle Points
5. Upgrade character: Spend BP on stat upgrades

Game Rules

Battle System:

  • Battle lasts 3 minutes
  • Player has 2000 HP, bot has 2400 HP
  • Normal attacks accumulate energy
  • Special attacks available at 100% energy
  • Defense reduces damage taken by 50%
  • Combo attacks give additional advantage

Economy:

  • BP - main in-game currency, earned for victories
  • Telegram Stars for NFT and accessory purchases
  • Referral system: 15 BP to new player, 40 BP to referrer
